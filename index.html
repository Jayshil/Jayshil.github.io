About Me

Shortly, I shall join the Department of Astronomy at Stockholm University as a PhD student in the group of Prof. Alexis Brandeker. I will work on understanding the nature of exoplanets by examining data from space-based missions TESS and CHEOPS. I will also assist in developing tools for analysing CHEOPS data.

Before moving to Stockholm, I was in Geneva learning Exoplanetary Science at Geneva Observatory in Switzerland. Even before that I was a undergraduate student at Sardar Vallabhbhai National Institute of Technology, Surat-7 in Gujarat, India.
