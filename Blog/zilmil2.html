Zilmil Sitaron ka Aangan Hoga... - Part 2

The announcement of Legislative Assembly Election in Gujarat was just done by ECI. Many candidates jumped in this election to try out their luck. In those 2 months, the state witnessed every kind of political drama by all the parties and their candidates. Many malcontent candidates changed their loyalty and shifted to the new political party as per new demanding situations. But when results announced, most of them drew a blank, failed to understand the mind of common people. Gujarat had never accepted those turncoat candidates.

One of them was Nikhil’s father. Before the election, his party gave the candidacy to his rival candidate by ignoring him. So, his father decided to change the party before the election, as the opposite party was agreed to give him candidacy of them. But unfortunately, his all calculations became wrong. He badly lost that election to his rival candidate, who was from his mother party. Even he lost his deposit and all the money that he spent during the election campaign was wasted. He was about to become bankrupt. He was very short tempered, and so decided to teach him a very good lesson.

Exactly in these times, Sanjana completed her MBBS. So, it’s time for celebration. Sanjana directly flew to Mumbai and met Nikhil. From many days her father asked her about her marriage. Both of theirs’s families were unknown about their relationship. Now, Sanjana thought that it is time to reveal her relationship with her parents. But before that, she met Nikhil and both of them decided to throw a party in celebration of the completion of her graduation. They had the plan to reveal their relationship in front of the whole crowd including parents of both.

“Sanjana, today I remembered the day when I proposed to you. Aha, awesome days were they! We were like on cloud nine, those days of our honeymoon period!”

“And how can I forget those your late night calls when you got admission in Delhi. That day only, I decided to take admission in the same college…”

“And you did that. Remembered that KFC tip, that I’d given to you when you selected for AIIMS, Delhi? Those two years, when we studied together, were some of the best years of my life…”

“Yes, of course. How can I forget that late night rambling on campus? And I became extremely happy when I thought that from some days later onward, we can live our long life together.” Both were very happy because they thought that very soon, they are going to marry. But no one knows what is there written in the destiny of them!

It was the day of Makar-Sankranti – kite flying festival. Almost all the people were on the terrace. Some of them were flying kite, some of them were holding firkis of their companion who was flying kites. Loud music was playing there on the terrace. Some people were just enjoying that music and watching the sky full of the kites. The wind was blowing at normal speed – good for flying kite. Sun was on its way to the horizon, quite near was already reached. The west direction became red with its redness. Darkness was rising in the atmosphere as the sun approached towards the horizon. But most of the people were still flying the kites regardless of the darkness. Two such persons were Nikhil and Sanjana. They both were on the terrace of their common friend. Both were flying kites side by side. Almost all people nearby took down their kites, but they were not in the mood to take down kites. Both were in the mood of cut down each other’s kite. Since a long time they were trying to do so, but as both are good kite-fighters, both were failing.

“Hey Nick, look here…”

“What’s the…” And his question remained incomplete, as he saw that Sanjana was giving him flying kiss. He was about to give an answer to her kiss, but suddenly…,

“A kai po che…” …he realised that it was a game to cut his kite.

Suddenly he realized something. Quickly he went nearby her, hold her firki and told,

“Sanjana, my kite went on its some infinite random journey.” He continued the conversation by looking straight into her beautiful eyes, “I also want to go on some journey. Would you like to come on travel with me?”

“Nikhil, first of all, this is not the time to discuss your journey, it’s time to celebrate my winning. And the second thing, which journey you are talking about? Where are you going?”

“Oh c’ mon! The destination doesn’t matter sometimes. And it’s the way towards the destination that makes you happy, not the destination. Travel is not all about the destination, it’s more about the way to the destination.”

“So?”

“So, would you like to travel with me on the journey of happiness, togetherness, love and many more such adventures, the journey that common people named as life? And yes, you want a destination, right? Listen, its destination will be the contentment of our lives.”

Wow! She was just amazed by the way that he proposed herself. She was not in the situation of breaking eye contact with him. She was blushing from inside. She had not imagined that he would propose her in this way. She had expected something different. Although there was a special type of feelings between them and both were aware of that, no one wanted to reveal that, because they feared that in doing so they would have lost another. Whenever they decided to meet, Sanjana always not to forget to make Nikhil’s favourite dish. Nikhil was master at organic chemistry, but Sanjana wasn’t. So Nikhil had taken the responsibility of her study of organic chemistry. Both were aware of their special feeling towards each other, but none of them wants to take initiative. But that day Nikhil took it. It was the time when for the first time Nikhil was so long away to his home and so Sanjana. Then only he realized the value of Sanjana in his life and decided to tell her about his feelings when first they meet.

“But promise me that you will never make me alone on this trip…” After breaking a quite long awkward silence, Sanjana indirectly accepted to travel with him.

Next after, it was another period of silence. Both were very emotional. Then Nikhil slowly came closer to her, threw the firki of her which he held in his hands, covered both sides of her face with his hands and then kissed her gently on her forehead. Sanjana in return hugged him tightly. Still, their eye contact was not broken. But their lips were not seemed to be satisfied with what they had done. Sanjana then put her hands on his shoulders and moved closer and his lips painted with the colour of lipsticks of her.

The sun and the moon were only witnesses of their union. The sun was already blushed with their union, and so its face became reddish and tried itself to hide behind some buildings. The moon was giving its blessings to this newly made couple from the zenith. Some tukkals were flying in the sky to give them blessings.

Today, when they were planning to reveal their relationship to their parents, Nikhil remembered that beautiful evening of Makar Sankranti.

“What happened Nikhil, are you OK?” When Sanjana saw that Nikhil wants to take rest during work time, because he was very punctual about his work and had never taken any rest during work time before it, except lunchtimes.

“Some weakness. I felt it for several days. It seems that my joints became stiff. Sometimes I felt like dizziness. I think that is due to the overload of work and irregular and unhygienic food…”

“Look at your belly. You becoming fat. Hit the gym, if suitable, otherwise, the gas trouble will increase. Surely it will due to an overload of work. Please, Nick, do not make your clinic the factory of printing currency notes, you earn enough. And hit the gym as soon as possible”

One doctor was giving advice to another! But destiny had something different for them! No one knows.

“Forget it. And let me tell that have completed all the arrangements for the party? And what about sending invitations?”

“I’d sent to everyone, except your parents. That task I’d handed over to you to do. Have sent an invitation to your parents?”

“My God! I forgot! How can I become such irresponsible? Damn it. Only two days are left to the party. Ok no problem, I’ll call them right now.”

Almost all of the arrangements for the upcoming party was completed. Now, Nikhil and Sanjana, both were waiting for the day.

That evening was glorious. The party management company had been completed its task very beautifully. As it was the party of becoming Sanjana a doctor, the theme for the party was also according to it. The stage was built like the ward of a hospital; the mic was of the shape of the stethoscope; instead of chairs, there were beds to sit; all of the guests had to wear aprons on their casuals. The hall was full of peoples. When Nikhil saw that parents of both of them had arrived, he thought that it would be better to do announcement first.

“Listen to everybody. Please come here, I have an announcement for all of you…” People started gathering after heard the announcement.

“So, for the last several months, my parents were asking me about my future, especially my marriage, because I had refused each and every girl. They were asking me whether I’ve a girlfriend or not, but I denied to tell them anything. I think, now, this is the time. So mom and dad, yes, I’m in a relationship. Please Sanjana, come forward… with this beautiful girl, my best friend, my fellow doctor and ex-classmate, Sanjana…”

“Yes dad, I’m in a relationship with him, that is why I was refusing each of your proposal for bridegroom…” Sanjana also told truth to his father.

The whole crowd cheered up for them. Almost everybody was happy with that couple. Their common friends became extremely happy to saw the transformation of two best friends into a couple. Further, both of them did observe the happiness on faces their parents. Everything was okay until then.

“Now, I would like to call my parents on the stage…” Nikhil invited his parents to the stage.

“And, please come forward dad, rather I should say newly elected MLA of our constituency so that we can acquaint both families…”

Oh my God! All contents for a big blast were there. An angry man, his rival, and furthermore, his son wants to marry that rival’s daughter. Yes, the rival candidate, from whom Nikhil’s father lost the election, on whom Nikhil’s father was very angry, was actually Sanjana’s father. Oh! Had I used the word Nikhil’s father? I should use Nikhil’s step-father.

“Oh, you? What the hell is this Nikhil? Have you fallen in love with the daughter to my rival? Sorry, but this can’t happen…”

“Oh, #&*# you. Who the hell you are to stopping me? You spherically b*@^$#^. How dare you to interrupt me? I saw you that day in fish-market. Just go there and sell fish. No one has the right to stop me…” Everybody was in shock by seeing that Nikhil was abusing his father, even Sanjana was in shock. She had never seen such a form of him. She tried to stop him, but that day, he was not in the mood to stop.

“You *&*#@%! #*#%*^&. Today you proved that you are not my blood. Though I fostered you, today you are pissing on me only. ^&^@#$*^#, now live on your own. I am going to sell my all properties which are in Bombay. And now onwards I see how can you survive?” Nikhil’s stepfather became furious when he saw that his step-son was abusing him for her girlfriend.

“@$%^ your property. I don’t even want it. Just go to #%^&*#%(#$ and $%^* someone. Or go to your high-command and lick their feet, you bloody turncoat…”

“Nikhil…” And a big heavy hand fell on his cheek. And in the reaction to it, Nikhil fell down and get unconscious. The whole crowd were left after seeing this duo of step-father-son. Sanjana was still in shock with Nikhil’s behaviour. After a while, nobody remained in the hall, except both of them. She was sobbing…

Truly, the destiny is Devil.
